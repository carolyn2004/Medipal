Question 1: What is Medipal?

+

Medipal is a medicine alarm clock designed for the elderly to ensure they take their medication. It sounds an alarm at set times, which only stops when the user opens the box and takes their medicine.

Question 2: What are the benefits of Medipal?

+

Medipal is an easy and useful device because it helps prevent missed doses and improves medication adherence. The elderly can easily determine the correct number of medication pills to take by viewing the information displayed on the LED screen of the Micro:bit. Additionally, they can locate misplaced medicine by following the sound levels emitted by the device. This combination of visual and auditory cues helps ensure they take the right dosage and find their medication with ease.

Question 3: Can I customize the alarm times and sounds on the Medipal?

+

Yes, Medipal allows you to customize alarm times to fit your medication schedule. You can also choose from different sounds for the alarm, making it more personal and easier for you to respond to the reminder.
